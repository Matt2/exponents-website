Outreach

Members of the exponents robotics team have reached out to promote our team and BEST Robotics. We have manned a booth at the One Day Academy book fair, as well as gave speeches in front of our other classes at One Day Academy.

We also brought the robot and fliers to Boy Scout troop 4031 and gave a presentation. Exponents continues to give presentation and speeches throughout robotics season and the rest of the year.
